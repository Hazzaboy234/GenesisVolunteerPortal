Open: Every weekday morning, from 10am-12pm

Location: Gateway Centre, London Road, Bath, BA1 6DH

Lifeline is a daily drop-in providing immediate support for people who are homeless, vulnerably housed or those experiencing complex problems, such as financial insecurity or drug and alcohol addiction.

It is a place of refuge, offering a warm and safe space with FREE hot breakfasts and drinks 5 mornings a week, emergency food parcels for rough sleepers, clothing, a shower and wash facilities. We also provide one-to-one support and advice to help people move from crisis, to stability and independence.

To donate clothing or volunteer please contact us. Or contact Lifeline directly on 01225 439384.
